Game Features

Various Character NPCs

In the game, you can encounter NPCs with diverse personalities. Engaging in conversations with them allows you to obtain additional game hints.

A variety of weapons.

In the game, every defeated enemy drops its corresponding weapon, and defeating powerful bosses grants access to rare weapons. Utilizing these potent weapons makes progressing through the game easier.
Alternatively, these weapons may also include effects such as burning, freezing, and more.

Maps with Various Styles

In the game, players can experience various map styles such as volcanoes, icy plains, and more. Each map features unique enemies,and, naturally, the weapons dropped by these enemies vary accordingly.
